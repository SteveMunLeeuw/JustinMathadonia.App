Help

 

When you open the app it shows your location on a map.  If the background shading is green, you are in a wet county meaning you can stock up on beer and wine at a grocerty store, get your cocktail supplies at a liquor store and go out to a bar.  If you find that you're in a red area you can't do any of that.  If the shading is somewhere in between click on the area and you'll get more information.  Sometimes you can buy liquor, sometimes it's the other way around and one state has an exemption if you're on  a golf course.
