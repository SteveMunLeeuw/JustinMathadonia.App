Beer-Run

Whether traveling or visiting friends figuring out where you can have a social drink can be a pain. Asking an uptight elderly person where the nearest bar is will rattle the steeliest of nerves.

A Google map with your location highlighting where you can and can't get a drink of alcohol. Whether you want a cocktail at a bar, a margarita or two with dinner, pick up a bottle of wine for a house warming or just grab a beer after that long bike ride. We are here to help.

Inspired on a spring break road trip through Arkansas. Who knew you couldn't just walk into a convenience store and buy a six-pack. If I save just one person a side trip to the Oklahoma border...
